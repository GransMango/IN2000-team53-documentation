Our Mission

WeatherApp is developed by Team 53 at the University of Oslo. Our aim is to provide real-time, accurate weather information to suggest the outdoor activities based on user-defined preferences for weather conditions. Users can customize alerts for wind speed, rainfall, and temperature for optimal outdoor experiences.

Development Approach

Throughout our agile development process we've combined Scrum and Kanban methodologies with regular updates, allowing for flexible yet disciplined project management. The app's design and development is powered by Android Studio and Jetpack Compose. For our APIs we're using Flask with continuous integration and deployment facilitated through GitHub Actions.

Meet the Team

Our team consists of dedicated software development students driven by a passion for creating solutions that bridge technology and practical usability. Our collective skills span across system architecture, UX design, and mobile app development.
